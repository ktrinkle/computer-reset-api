Welcome to the Computer Reset Liquidation Signup Site!

We are a group of volunteers helping the daughter of Richard Byron, owner of Computer Reset, liquidate the Dallas legendary computer resale shop.

Located within the walls of Computer Reset are a large collection of IBM PC Convertible parts, IBM PCJr computers and accessories, and numerous IBM and clone computers spanning 30+ years. Other items that have been sold include the famous Dune Buggy, cars, audio/visual equipment, a fire hydrant, a boat motor, and all sorts of technology-related items.

Liquidation events are held when announced, as permitted by weather and volunteer availability. Event signups are offered for a specific date and time slot, and are only available in limited numbers to comply with social distancing and governmental requirements.

All proceeds of the sales benefit the Byron family estate.

Official Computer Reset merchandise can be found on Amazon at this link. Only merchandise from this seller (Retro-Hi) benefits the Byron family. Any items on eBay or other Amazon sellers are counterfeit!
